3 | Who is she?
她是谁？

她是谁？

Tā shì shéi?

Who is she?

她是明美。

Tā shì míngměi.

She is Ming Mei.

她是韩国人吗？

Tā shì hánguórén ma?

Is she Korean?

不是。她是日本人。

Búshì. Tā shì rìběnrén.

No. She is Japanese.

哦，她是老师吗？

Ò, tā shì lǎoshī ma?

Oh. Is she a teacher?

不是，她不是老师。她是学生。

Búshì, tā búshì lǎoshī. Tā shì xuésheng.

No. She is not a teacher. She is a student.

谁是老师？

Shéi shì lǎoshī?

Who is the teacher?

他是老师。

Tā shì lǎoshī.

He is the teacher
